Regression Model for predicting DELTA

Problem Definition
  • The Dataset consists of 7 Columns.
  • The target is IRIF(in/mile).
  • Features are (IRI0(in/mile), AGE, FC%, LC(ft/mile), TC(ft/mile), RUT(in)).
  • The Distribution of (IRI0 & RIF(in/mile)) Columns is exponential Distribution, taking the natural log(value) to optimize the Distribution to be Normal, and after Model predicts take (exponential(value)) to reverse.
  • The Distribution of (AGE & RUT(in)) Columns is also exponential Distribution, using box-cox tramsformation to optimize the Distribution to be Normal Distribution is better to help the Model learn and train correctly.
  • Training KNN & RF as our Baseline Models
  • The best Algorithm is (GBRT) an algorithm based on Ensemble Learning Understand GBRT
  • The Algorithm results are -- r2_score = 85.2 % , MAE = 3.57, MSE = 59.35 -- on validation Dataset.
